# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Summary
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze car sales performance, monitor key business KPIs, and provide actionable insights through dynamic visualizations and filters.
## 🔍 Overview
The Car Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, average selling price, and vehicle sales trends. It enables stakeholders to track Year-to-Date (YTD) and Month-to-Date (MTD) metrics, compare performance with previous periods, and identify growth opportunities.
## ❓ Problem Statement
A car dealership requires a centralized dashboard to monitor sales performance across different vehicle brands, body styles, colors, dealer regions, and time periods. Analyzing raw sales data manually is time-consuming and makes it difficult to identify trends and business opportunities.
## 🗂️ Dataset
The dataset contains car sales transaction data, including:

- Car ID
- Date
- Customer Name
- Dealer Name
- Company Model
- Body Style
- Color
- Transmission
- Engine Type
- Dealer Region
- Sales Amount
## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
## ⚙️ Method
1. Imported and cleaned raw sales data using Power Query.
2. Created a date table and established data model relationships.
3. Developed DAX measures for YTD, MTD, YOY Growth, and KPI calculations.
4. Built interactive visualizations and KPI cards.
5. Implemented slicers for Body Style, Dealer Name, Transmission, and Engine.
6. Designed Overview and Details pages for high-level and detailed analysis.
## 💡 Key Insights
- YTD Total Sales reached $371.2M.
- Sales increased by 23.59% compared to the previous year.
- YTD Average Selling Price was $28.0K.
- Total Cars Sold YTD reached 13.3K units.
- SUVs contributed the largest share of total sales by body style.
- Pale White emerged as the top-selling vehicle color.
- Dealer regions showed varying sales performance, highlighting high-performing markets.
- Company-wise analysis identified top-performing automotive brands.
- Weekly sales trend analysis helped track business growth patterns throughout the year.
